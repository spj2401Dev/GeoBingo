Challenge friends to search for prompts in real life

Geobingo is an open-source game where you and your friends are assigned prompts to find and photograph objects in the real world.

Contribute on Github!

We are Open Source ♥. Star the Repo on Github. Over there you can find instructions on how to selfhost and play for yourselves

Features:

  • 🤝 Teams are supported. Play with multiple people together
  • 📱 Built from the ground up for mobile
  • 📸 Voting. Give extra points to the best photos
  • 🔒 Self-hosted. No need to sign up. Just create a room and play. All the data will be stored on your own servers
  • 🔍 Admin Features: remove pictures that do not follow the prompt
  • 🗜️ Compression built-in. Save data when on the run
  • 💻 Good looking UI (if I do say so myself)
