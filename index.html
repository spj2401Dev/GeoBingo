Set your own Prompts

Every game you can define your own custom prompt or you can store already working ones in the Worldlist, to easily access when you don't know what words to pick

Play alone or in Teams

Joining made simple with a large QR Code at the top of the page. Choose to play in Teams or alone.

Take photos

When the game is running you will see all the words you will have to complete, the time left and how many words you have completed in Total.

Retake Photos at any Time

Completed Photos move to the very bottom, to save space. If you are not happy with a photo, you can always retake it.

View all your Pictures

After the game is over, you will be able to see everyones Pictures to any prompt they took a picture of.

Vote for and remove pictures

In the End, every Player is able to Vote for the best Pictures. Every vote will add a one point to a players total score. Players willnot be able to vote for themselves.
